LOG.01 // OPERATOR PROFILE

The Build Behind the Model

STATUS: ACTIVE

I'm Alexandra — currently pursuing my MCA in Data Science at the University of Allahabad, and building things in the space between raw data and working software. My tools are Python, scikit-learn, TensorFlow and PyTorch; my obsession is turning messy real-world data into pipelines that actually hold up in production.

I published pycleanframe to PyPI — a decorator-based library that keeps pandas DataFrames clean before they ever reach your function. I've trained classifiers on 10,000+ equipment records, built NLP pipelines that cut recruiter review time by 60%, and shipped a full-stack vaccination tracker that placed top 5 out of 198 teams at UHACK 3.0.
